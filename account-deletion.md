# Account Deletion
 
**ScentPass**
Last updated: June 17, 2026
 
---
 
## How to delete your account
 
**If you have the app installed:**
Open ScentPass, go to **Settings → Delete Account**, and confirm. Your account and all associated data are deleted permanently and immediately.
 
**If you don't have access to the app:**
Email **scentpass.support@gmail.com** from the email address associated with your ScentPass account, with the subject line "Delete my account." Once we verify your request, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days, consistent with our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy).
 
---
 
## What data is deleted
 
- Your name and email address
- Your olfactory profile (favorite fragrance families, occasions, signature scent)
- Your personal fragrance collection (fragrances you own, have tried, or want to try)
- Your personal ratings for fragrances
---
 
## What data is retained, and for how long
 
We do not retain any personal data beyond the 30-day window described above. If you used ScentPass in guest mode (without an account), no data was ever stored on our servers, so there is nothing to delete remotely.
